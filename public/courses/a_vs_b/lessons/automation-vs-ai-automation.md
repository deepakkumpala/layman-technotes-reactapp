
### Automation

Automation means using technology to do tasks on its own, without human help.
It works by having a trigger (something that starts it) and an action (what it does). You can have just one action or a chain of triggers and actions.

---

### AI Automation

AI Automation is the same idea, but now there's a brain , a Large Language Model (LLM), that can understand and decide what to do.
